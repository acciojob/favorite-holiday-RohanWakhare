Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto is the cultural heart of Japan, home to over a thousand temples, traditional wooden machiya townhouses, and some of the most breathtaking gardens in the world. Once the imperial capital, it perfectly blends ancient history with modern Japanese life.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Fushimi Inari Shrine — famous for its thousands of torii gates
  2. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove — a magical walk through towering bamboo
  3. Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) — a stunning gold-covered Zen temple
  4. Gion District — the historic geisha quarter of Kyoto
  5. Philosopher's Path — a scenic canal-side walk lined with cherry trees

Must-Try Foods

  • Kaiseki — a traditional multi-course Japanese dinner
  • Matcha sweets — rich green tea flavored cakes and ice cream
  • Yudofu — delicate tofu hotpot served in temple restaurants
  • Nishin Soba — buckwheat noodles topped with sweet herring
  • Obanzai — home-style Kyoto cooking with seasonal vegetables
